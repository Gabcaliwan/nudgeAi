Product Features

Everything Nudge AI does.

7 core features, designed to work together as one seamless experience.

🧠
Smart Nudge System
Real-time, context-aware suggestions triggered by time, inactivity, behavior patterns, and your own goals. Never commands — always suggestions.
🎯
Goal Tracking
Simple progress bars and streak tracking for up to 5 goals. No complex setups — just set a goal and the AI tracks behavior toward it automatically.
📅
Habit Awareness
Light daily habit check-ins (done/not done). The AI notices patterns and nudges you when you're falling behind or about to break a streak.
⏱
Time Awareness
Detects free time windows using calendar integration and screen behavior. Proactively suggests productive uses for idle blocks of 30 minutes or more.
💸
Spending Awareness
Optional manual logging or lightweight bank API integration. AI cross-references purchases against your savings goals and nudges before or during impulse buys.
🔮
Future You Preview
"If you keep this up, in 30 days you'll have saved $240 extra." Consequence-of-action previews shown when the AI detects a potential off-track decision.
🔔
Smart Notifications
AI learns your "low resistance" windows (post-lunch, evenings) and sends nudges only then. Max 3 per day to avoid notification fatigue.
📍
Live Location & Traffic
Monitors real-time traffic on your saved routes and cross-references your calendar. Nudges you before you need to leave — "Traffic is heavy, leave in 8 min or you'll be late." Also detects nearby distractions (coffee shops, malls) when you're trying to save money.
🔥
Soft Roast Mode
"You opened the fridge 4 times in 20 minutes. The food isn't going to change. Bestie." Shareable cards optimized for TikTok/Reels.
📋
Weekly Reality Check
Every Sunday: a shareable summary card with your week in numbers, your top nudge, and a 1-line AI assessment. Designed to be posted.
AI Personalities

Three voices. One goal.

😌
Chill
"Hey, no pressure, but you've got like 40 free minutes. Maybe start that thing you've been putting off? Or don't, I'm just saying."
Warm, low-pressure, conversational. Perfect for users who hate being bossed around. Default personality.
💪
Coach
"You've been working toward this goal for 12 days. That's real. This moment — right now — is where people either quit or make it. Which one are you?"
Motivational, high-energy, goal-obsessed. Best for users who want accountability and like being pushed.
🔥
Savage
"Bro you've checked your ex's Instagram 6 times today and not opened your notes app once. We need to talk."
Funny, slightly harsh, highly shareable. Designed to go viral. Ideal for Gen Z who want entertainment with accountability.

🚦 Leave-Now Alerts

Nudge AI pulls your next calendar event's address, calculates current ETA using Google Maps Traffic API, and alerts you when it's time to leave — factoring in your personal "buffer time" preference (5, 10, or 15 min early). Updates every 5 minutes if you haven't left yet.

🗺️ Alternate Route Suggestions

When traffic is detected, the AI doesn't just warn you — it immediately calculates the best alternative route and shows a side-by-side time comparison. One tap to open in Apple Maps or Google Maps.

📍 Geofence Spending Alerts

User sets "spending zones" (malls, specific streets, restaurant areas). When GPS detects you're within ~0.3 miles of a zone and you have an active savings goal, a gentle nudge fires. Works passively in the background — no need to open the app.

🏠 Commute Learning

After 2 weeks of use, the AI learns your typical commute patterns (when you usually leave, which routes you take, how long they take). It builds a personal baseline and flags anomalies — "You usually leave at 8:30 but traffic is 2x worse on Fridays. Leave by 8:10 today."

🔋 Battery & Privacy Notes

Background location uses significant-change mode (not continuous GPS) to preserve battery — only polls precise location when a calendar event is within 2 hours. Location data is processed on-device; only anonymized ETA calculations hit external APIs. Users can disable at any time.

Technical Design

AI System Architecture.

How the intelligence behind Nudge AI actually works — kept practical and buildable.

Data Layer
InputUser Behavior Signals

Screen time (iOS Screen Time / Android Digital Wellbeing API), calendar free/busy slots, manual habit check-ins, optional spending logs, app usage timestamps, time of day patterns, real-time GPS location, saved routes, Google Maps traffic data, geofence events (entering/leaving zones).

↓
ProcessingLocal Context Engine

On-device lightweight ML (Core ML / TFLite) builds a behavioral profile: peak distraction windows, streak data, goal progress delta, spending velocity. Privacy-first — raw data stays on device.

↓
AINudge Generation Layer

Context payload sent to GPT-4o mini or Claude Haiku API with a system prompt that defines the selected personality, active goals, current behavioral state, and nudge trigger type. Output: a single, short nudge message.

↓
OutputNudge Delivery System

Smart notification scheduling via local push (no background server needed). Nudge delivered as push notification + stored in feed. Fatigue prevention: max 3 nudges/day, DND windows enforced.

Nudge Trigger Logic
⏱ Time Trigger

Free calendar window ≥30 min detected → nudge with task suggestion based on highest-priority pending goal.

📱 Inactivity / Scroll Trigger

Screen time on entertainment apps (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) exceeds 30 min without break → nudge.

💸 Spending Trigger

Purchase event logged that exceeds daily spend budget OR category conflict with active savings goal detected → pre-purchase nudge.

🌙 Streak / Habit Trigger

Approaching end of day without completing a habit that has a 3+ day active streak → gentle reminder before midnight.

🔮 Future You Trigger

Behavior deviation from goal detected 3 days in a row → projection summary showing current trajectory vs goal outcome.

NEW 📍 Location & Traffic Trigger

Background location + Google Maps Traffic API monitors saved routes continuously. Cross-references next calendar event's location + current ETA. If ETA ≥ event start time minus buffer (user-defined, default 10 min) → urgent leave-now nudge with alternate route suggestion. Also triggers a spending nudge if user's GPS shows they've stopped near a shopping zone while on a savings goal day.

Viral Strategy

Built to spread.

Three features designed specifically to generate organic social sharing.

🔥 Soft Roast Mode
Savage AI personality generates witty, relatable callouts about user behavior. Each roast is a shareable card with the Nudge AI watermark. Designed for TikTok and Instagram Stories.

Example: "You've opened Uber Eats 5 times today and closed it without ordering. That's not indecision — that's a whole character arc."
📊 Shareable Nudges
Every nudge in the feed has a "Share" button that generates a stylized card (9:16 format for Stories, 1:1 for feed). The card shows the nudge text, your streak, and a subtle Nudge AI logo.

Hook: People sharing their roasts with friends creates massive word-of-mouth install loops.
🧾 Weekly Reality Check
Every Sunday, a full-screen summary card is generated: "You scrolled 14 hours, saved $60, and kept 4/7 habits." One-tap share to any platform.

Virality mechanic: The mild shame/pride combination makes these impossible not to share. Think Spotify Wrapped but weekly and brutally honest.

👥 Target User
Ages 18–28. College students, early-career professionals. Already use Notion, Duolingo, or Finch. Frustrated by existing productivity apps feeling "too much." Extremely online — TikTok, Instagram. Care about money, time, and self-improvement but hate being lectured.
⚙️ MVP Tech Choices
Framework: React Native + Expo (fastest cross-platform)
AI: GPT-4o mini (cheapest per nudge, ~$0.0001/call)
Backend: Firebase (auth, Firestore for nudge history)
Payments: RevenueCat (5 min setup, handles all IAP)
Push: Expo Notifications (free, reliable)
Analytics: PostHog (free tier, great for mobile funnels)
